Frequently Asked Questions
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Contact our team to initiate a return.
How long does shipping take? ▼
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days depending on the destination country.
Do you offer international shipping? ▼
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your location.
Can I modify or cancel my order? ▼
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters processing and changes may not be possible. Contact us immediately if needed.
